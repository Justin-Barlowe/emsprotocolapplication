Site Launched! 10/31/2023

I am pleased to announce the launch of the Harnett County EMS Protocols website. This platform aims to centralize all Policies, Protocols, and Procedures, making them easily accessible to our providers. I am committed to continuously enhancing the site, and in the coming months, you can anticipate a series of critical updates and new features. Additionally, I am in the process of developing a mobile application that will function offline. Should you have any questions, feedback, or suggestions, I welcome you to reach out to me at Justin.Barlowe@proton.me. I will respond as quickly as I can.
